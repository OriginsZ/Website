Server Details

  • Version: Minecraft 1.20.2 (Fabric)
  • IP Address: play.originsz.xyz
  • Offline Mode: off

How to Connect

To join the OriginsZ Minecraft Server, follow these steps:

  1. Open Minecraft and go to the "Multiplayer" menu.
  2. Click "Add Server."
  3. Enter the server IP address: play.originsz.xyz
  4. Click "Done" and then select the server from your list to join.

You can only enter the server if you are whitelisted!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHITELISTING ‎‎

How do I get whitelisted?

To get whitelisted, you need to submit an application through our Discord server or contact a staff member. Ensure you follow all the guidelines provided in the application form.

What is the whitelist process?

Once you submit your application, it will be reviewed by our staff. If approved, you will be added to the whitelist and notified accordingly.

STAFF APPLYING ‎‎

How can I apply for staff?

To apply for a staff position, please send us a message through our Discord server with your application. Include your previous experience, availability, and why you want to join our team.

What are the requirements for staff?

We look for dedicated and responsible individuals who have a good understanding of our server rules and have been an active part of the community. Previous moderation experience is a plus.
